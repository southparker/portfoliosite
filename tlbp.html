The Little Bird Project System
Drawn slightly wonky to give the logo individuality and character. The unevenness and hand done aesthetic echoes the overall visual language of the project. The two hands holding symbolize healing, support, and connection. Hands were chosen as the main graphic element because they are relatable and human. The colors were bright and arresting in order to echo the uncomfortable subject of sexual assault and rape. However the bright colors also make it easier to approach the topic. The typography and hands were hand drawn to play off the visual metaphor.
